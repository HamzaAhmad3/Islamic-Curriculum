Zakariyya al-Ansari's extensive commentary on Rawd al-Talib, a substantial multi-volume reference work that became an essential source for his own students — including Ibn Hajar al-Haytami and Shams al-Din al-Ramli — who went on to define much of the school's later mu'tamad positions.
