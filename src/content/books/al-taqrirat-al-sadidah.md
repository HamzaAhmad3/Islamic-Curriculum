Takes the entirety of beginner/intermediate Shafi'i fiqh and organizes it into charts, bullet points, conditions, and pillars — visually clear and designed to solve the "clutter" problem in a student's mind. Recommended as a supplement alongside Masa'il al-Ta'lim (al-Muqaddimah al-Hadramiyyah).
