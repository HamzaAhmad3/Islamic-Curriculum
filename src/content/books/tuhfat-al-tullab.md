Zakariyya al-Ansari's self-commentary on his own abridgement, Tahrir Tanqih al-Lubab — in practice, students read Tuhfat al-Tullab itself as the primary text, since al-Ansari's own explanation is folded directly into it. Covers purification, prayer, funerals, and zakat among other topics, with the school's relied-upon positions presented alongside their primary evidences and reasoning.
