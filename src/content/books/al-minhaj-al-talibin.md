Imam al-Nawawi's advanced legal manual, condensed from al-Rafi'i's al-Muharrar. Minhaj al-Talibin became the central reference point of the later Shafi'i school — its many commentaries, most notably by al-Ramli and Ibn Hajar al-Haytami, effectively define the school's modern relied-upon positions.
