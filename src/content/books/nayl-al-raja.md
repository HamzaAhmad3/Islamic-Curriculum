Written in al-Shatiri's trademark style — crisp, organized, and modern language. Where older commentaries like Kashifat as-Saja focus on deep linguistic breakdowns, Nayl al-Raja focuses purely on ensuring the student practically understands the fiqh of the Safinah with zero confusion.
