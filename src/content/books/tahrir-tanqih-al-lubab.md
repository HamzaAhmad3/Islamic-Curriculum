An intermediate Shafi'i legal primer, abridged by Zakariyya al-Ansari from his teacher Wali al-Din al-'Iraqi's Tanqih al-Lubab. Al-Ansari then wrote his own commentary on this abridgement, Tuhfat al-Tullab, which became the standard way the text is actually studied.
