A dense, comprehensive Shafi'i legal manual, widely known in English through Sheikh Nuh Ha Mim Keller's translation. Several major shuruh exist at different depths, from the accessible (Anwar al-Masalik) to the exhaustively technical (Fath al-Wahhab al-Malik, a multi-volume modern reference).
