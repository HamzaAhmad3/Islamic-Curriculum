A foundational beginner-level matn (primer) in Shafi'i fiqh, widely used as an early text after the
shorter Safinat an-Najaa pathway. Several major commentaries exist at different depths, from the
purely definitional (Fath al-Qareeb) to the evidence-based (Kifayat al-Akhyar) to the exhaustively
comprehensive (al-Iqna').
