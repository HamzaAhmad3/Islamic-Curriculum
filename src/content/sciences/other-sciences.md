Several smaller disciplines — Aqidah, Mustalah al-Hadith, Tasawwuf, and lexicon (Ma'ajim) works — are
grouped here for now. As each grows, it is likely to be promoted into its own dedicated science page.

Note: Adab and Seerah are not treated as curriculum sciences here — they don't carry a pathway or
progression of their own. They appear only as personal reading items on the [Dashboard](/dashboard),
not as catalog entries.
