Ibn al-Salah taught in Damascus and produced what is widely regarded as the founding systematic text of hadith terminology as an organized discipline. Nearly every subsequent work in Mustalah al-Hadith — including Ibn Hajar al-'Asqalani's Nukhbat al-Fikr — engages with, summarizes, or responds to the categories Ibn al-Salah first laid out.
