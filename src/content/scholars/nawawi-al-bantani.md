Muhammad Nawawi al-Bantani left Java as a young man to study and eventually teach in Mecca, becoming one of the most published Southeast Asian scholars of his era despite writing far from his region of origin. His commentary on Safinat an-Naja, Kashifat us-Saja, reflects his broader inclination to blend precise legal explanation with the spiritual dimension of practice.
