Al-Shatiri's Nayl al-Raja on Safinat an-Naja reflects his broader reputation for organized, modern presentation — where older commentaries like Kashifat as-Saja dwell on linguistic analysis, al-Shatiri focuses purely on making sure the beginning student walks away with a working, confusion-free grasp of the fiqh.
