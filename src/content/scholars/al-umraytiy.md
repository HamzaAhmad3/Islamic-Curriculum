Al-'Umraytiy specialized in a particular pedagogical craft: taking established prose primers and re-rendering them in verse, on the theory that rhythm and rhyme make memorization far more durable than prose ever could. His versifications of both al-Ajrumiyyah and al-Waraqat are still used for exactly that purpose today.
