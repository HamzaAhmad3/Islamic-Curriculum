Zayn al-Din al-Malibari studied in the Hejaz under Ibn Hajar al-Haytami before returning to India, where his Fath al-Mu'in became — and largely remains — the dominant reference text for Shafi'i legal practice across South and Southeast Asia, a region far from the school's historical Middle Eastern centers.
