Mustafa al-Khinn taught Islamic law at Damascus University and, together with Mustafa al-Bugha and Ali al-Sharbaji, co-authored al-Fiqh al-Manhaji — a three-volume intermediate work that became one of the most widely assigned modern textbooks in the Shafi'i school, prized for its clarity and grounding in evidence.
