Muhammad Sayyid Tantawi served first as Grand Mufti of Egypt and later as Shaykh al-Azhar, among the most senior religious authorities in the Sunni world. His al-Tafsir al-Wasit offers a modern comprehensive treatment of the Qur'an from within that mainstream institutional tradition.
