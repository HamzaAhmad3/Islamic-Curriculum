Taqi al-Din al-Hisni taught in Damascus and became known above all for a single, enduringly popular work: Kifayat al-Akhyar, a commentary on Matn Abi Shuja' distinguished by its consistent practice of citing the Qur'anic verses and hadith behind each ruling — still recommended today as the natural next step for a student who has learned the bare mechanics of the law and now wants to know why.
