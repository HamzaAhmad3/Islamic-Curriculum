Al-Shatibi went blind early in life yet became the foremost authority on the seven canonical Qur'anic readings (qira'at) of his generation, versifying the entire discipline into al-Shatibiyyah — a poem still memorized in full by specialists in Qira'at today, and considered close to the definitive reference on the subject.
