Jalal al-Din al-Mahalli began Tafsir al-Jalalayn but died before completing it; his student Jalal al-Din al-Suyuti finished the remainder so seamlessly that the work is still read today as a single continuous text under both their names. Beyond tafsir, al-Mahalli was a formidable commentator in his own right — his Sharh al-Waraqat remains a standard intermediate companion to al-Juwayni's primer, and his commentary on Jam' al-Jawami' caps the advanced Usul al-Fiqh curriculum.
