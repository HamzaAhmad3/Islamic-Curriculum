Al-Saqqaf's Tarshih al-Mustafidin is deliberately narrower in scope than I'anat al-Talibin — rather than covering everything, it focuses specifically on the points where Fath al-Mu'in's text differs from the school's standard mu'tamad positions, making it a favored comparative reference for advanced students.
