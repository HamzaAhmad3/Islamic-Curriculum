Ibn al-Jazari is widely regarded as the last great consolidating authority in the science of Qur'anic recitation, extending the canonical readings from seven to ten and systematizing the discipline's rules in a form still taught essentially unchanged today. His Muqaddimah al-Jazariyyah remains the standard next step after Tuhfat al-Atfal in the Tajwid curriculum worldwide.
