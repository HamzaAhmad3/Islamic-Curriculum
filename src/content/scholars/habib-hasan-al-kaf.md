Habib Hasan bin Ahmad al-Kaf, working within the Tarim scholarly tradition, produced al-Taqrirat al-Sadidah — a modern work so highly regarded that it is commonly recommended as the single book to own if a student can only afford one beyond the classical curriculum, organizing beginner and intermediate fiqh into clear charts, conditions, and pillars.
