Jamal al-Din Ibn Hisham is often ranked among the two or three greatest Arabic grammarians of any era. His output was unusual in spanning every level of the discipline at once: Qatr al-Nada and Shudhur al-Dhahab remain standard intermediate texts, while Mughni al-Labib is an encyclopedic advanced reference still consulted by specialists. He also wrote his own commentary on Ibn Malik's Alfiyyah, Awdah al-Masalik, one of the two most-read commentaries on that poem alongside Ibn 'Aqil's.
