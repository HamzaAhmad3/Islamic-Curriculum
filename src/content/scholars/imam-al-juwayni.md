Abu al-Ma'ali al-Juwayni earned the honorific "Imam al-Haramayn" for years spent teaching in the two holiest cities before returning to Nishapur, where he taught the young Abu Hamid al-Ghazali. His short primer al-Waraqat remains, to this day, the very first text nearly every student of Usul al-Fiqh in the Shafi'i tradition encounters, and his Nihayat al-Matlab is still cited as a key reference for understanding the deeper reasoning (ta'lil) behind fiqh rulings.
