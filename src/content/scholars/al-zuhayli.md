Wahbah al-Zuhayli taught at Damascus University for decades and produced an extraordinary volume of scholarship, most famously his multi-volume comparative encyclopedia of fiqh across all major schools. His shorter works — al-Wajiz in Usul al-Fiqh and al-Tafsir al-Wajiz — serve as accessible, university-level entry points into subjects usually approached only through denser classical texts.
