Ibn Daqiq al-'Id served as Chief Judge of Egypt and brought a jurist's precision to his commentary on al-Nawawi's Forty Hadith — deliberately brief, extracting the core legal rulings (ahkam) and linguistic points from each hadith without extended digression, making it a natural first commentary for students of the collection.
