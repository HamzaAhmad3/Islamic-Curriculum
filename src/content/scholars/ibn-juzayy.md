Ibn Juzayy wrote from al-Andalus in its final century as a center of Islamic scholarship, and his Taqrib al-Wusul is still singled out among classical Usul al-Fiqh texts for how logically and clearly it divides its subject matter — later students and teachers regularly point to it as the easiest advanced-track text in the discipline to actually outline.
