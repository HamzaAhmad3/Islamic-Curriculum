Abu al-Su'ud served as Shaykh al-Islam of the Ottoman Empire for nearly three decades under Suleiman the Magnificent, one of the longest and most influential tenures in that position's history. His tafsir is particularly noted among specialists for its attentiveness to the Qur'an's rhetorical craft.
