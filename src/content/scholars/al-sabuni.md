Muhammad Ali al-Sabuni taught for many years in Mecca and produced Safwat al-Tafasir specifically to condense and clarify the classical tafsir tradition for a broad modern readership — still commonly recommended as an early full read-through of the Qur'an before moving to denser classical works.
