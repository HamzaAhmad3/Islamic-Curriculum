A widely published 20th-century scholar at al-Azhar, Muhyi al-Din Abdul Hamid produced accessible modern commentaries and critical editions of several classical Nahw staples, making his editions of Qatr al-Nada and Shudhur al-Dhahab a common sight in Arabic grammar classrooms to this day.
