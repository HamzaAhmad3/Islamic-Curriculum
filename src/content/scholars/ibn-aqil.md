Baha' al-Din Ibn 'Aqil's commentary on the Alfiyyah has, across seven centuries, become close to inseparable from the poem itself — for most students, "reading the Alfiyyah" in practice means reading it through Ibn 'Aqil's explanation, alongside Ibn Hisham's competing Awdah al-Masalik.
