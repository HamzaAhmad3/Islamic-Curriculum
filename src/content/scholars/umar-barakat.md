Umar Barakat's commentary on 'Umdat as-Salik became, somewhat by historical accident, one of the most-read pieces of Shafi'i fiqh scholarship in the English-speaking world — Shaykh Nuh Ha Mim Keller's 20th-century translation, Reliance of the Traveller, is built directly on Barakat's text.
