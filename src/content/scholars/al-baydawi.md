Al-Baydawi served as a judge in Shiraz and wrote two of the most compressed, commentary-dependent texts in their respective fields: Minhaj al-Wusul in Usul al-Fiqh and his tafsir, both strictly following the Mutakallimun method and packing enormous debate into minimal words — genuinely difficult to approach without a teacher or an expansive commentary alongside.
