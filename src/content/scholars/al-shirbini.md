Muhammad al-Khatib al-Shirbini taught and wrote in Cairo during the tenth Islamic century, producing legal commentaries that remain in active use today for their combination of comprehensiveness and readability — his al-Iqna' on Matn Abi Shuja' is still commonly assigned to advanced students working through the text in full.
