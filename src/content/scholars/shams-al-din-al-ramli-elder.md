Shihab al-Din al-Ramli laid the scholarly groundwork — in the form of his own extensive work, Fath al-Rahman — that his son Shams al-Din al-Ramli later drew on to write Ghayat al-Bayan, the standard authoritative commentary on Safwat al-Zubad.
