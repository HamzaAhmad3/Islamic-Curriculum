Al-'Aytah's Fiqh al-'Ibadah summarizes the teaching of Shaykh 'Abd al-Karim al-Rifa'i's classes on the Syrian curriculum — a work still difficult to find outside Damascus, but well regarded for its clear presentation, including unusually thorough coverage of female purification issues.
