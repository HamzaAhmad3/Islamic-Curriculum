Hasan ibn Ali al-Kafrawi al-Azhari al-Shafi'i taught at al-Azhar in Cairo. His commentary on al-Ajrumiyyah has remained a standard beginner-level companion to the primer for generations of students taking their first steps into Arabic grammar, still printed in multiple modern editions.
