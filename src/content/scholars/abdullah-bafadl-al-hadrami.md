Abdullah Bafadl al-Hadrami wrote al-Muqaddimah al-Hadramiyyah as a focused primer on the acts of worship ('Ibadat) alone, deliberately narrower in scope than a full beginner matn — a decision that let it become the standard first substantial fiqh text across Yemen and the Hejaz, still assigned today before students move on to broader works like Matn Abi Shuja'.
