Al-Tirmidhi studied under al-Bukhari and went on to compile one of the six canonically recognized hadith collections, distinguished among them for regularly noting the strength or weakness of each hadith it contains. His al-Shama'il, a focused collection describing the Prophet Muhammad's appearance, habits, and character, remains the standard starting point for that specific genre of study.
