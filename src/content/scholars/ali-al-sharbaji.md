Ali al-Sharbaji is the third co-author, alongside Mustafa al-Khinn and Mustafa al-Bugha, of al-Fiqh al-Manhaji — the widely used intermediate Shafi'i textbook based on Mughni al-Muhtaj that has introduced generations of Arabic-speaking students to evidence-based fiqh.
