Ibn Rajab al-Hanbali, despite belonging to the Hanbali school himself, wrote the commentary on al-Nawawi's Shafi'i-associated Forty Hadith that is now read across all schools — Jami' al-'Ulum wa al-Hikam expands the original 42 hadiths to 50 and treats each with a depth of spiritual and legal reflection that has made it a classic in its own right, independent of the base text it comments on.
