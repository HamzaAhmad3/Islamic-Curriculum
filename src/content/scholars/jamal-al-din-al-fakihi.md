Al-Fakihi's Mujib al-Nida goes well beyond teaching students how to speak correctly — it takes up the historic debates between the Basran and Kufan grammatical schools, citing classical poetry as evidence, aimed at advanced students ready to engage with grammar as a subject of genuine scholarly dispute rather than settled rules.
