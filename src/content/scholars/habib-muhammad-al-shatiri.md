Habib Muhammad taught his father's text, al-Yaqut al-Nafis, for years in Tarim; the resulting commentary — compiled from his students' transcriptions of those lessons — is distinctive for illustrating classical rulings with thoroughly modern examples, reading more like a contemporary lecture than a traditional gloss.
