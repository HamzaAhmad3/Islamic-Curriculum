Al-Dimyati's I'anat al-Talibin is what makes Fath al-Mu'in practically teachable — it breaks the base text's dense syntax down explicitly and updates the rulings throughout with the final positions of al-Ramli and Ibn Hajar al-Haytami, becoming essential for anyone actually studying Fath al-Mu'in rather than simply consulting it.
