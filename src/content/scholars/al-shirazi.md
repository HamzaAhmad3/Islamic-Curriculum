Abu Ishaq al-Shirazi taught at the Nizamiyyah madrasa in Baghdad and wrote al-Luma', an intermediate Usul al-Fiqh text distinguished by its practical focus — unlike many later works in the discipline, it largely avoids theological (kalam) tangents in favor of material a working jurist can actually use.
