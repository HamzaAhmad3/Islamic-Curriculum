Al-Hattab al-Ru'ayni's Qurrat al-'Ayn has served for centuries as the default first commentary on al-Juwayni's al-Waraqat, prized for simplifying the primer's dense definitions into sentences a beginning student can follow without losing the precision the subject demands.
